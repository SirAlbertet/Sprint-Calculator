Who We Are

Two athletes.
One idea.

We are Albert and Adrià — two high-level sprinters from Barcelona and Computer Science students who became obsessed with one question: why is there no reliable tool to predict sprint performance?

We train, compete, and study the sport every day. Sprint Calculator was born out of that obsession — combining our technical background with our experience on the track to build something athletes can actually trust.

How It Works

The model
behind it.

Sprint Calculator combines two complementary techniques. For single-input cases — such as converting a hand-timed mark to an electronic equivalent — we use simple linear regression, optimised for precision on sprint-specific data.

For multi-input predictions, we use Extreme Gradient Boosting (XGBoost), trained on a database of approximately 10,000 official 100m and 200m races. The result accurately distinguishes all types of sprinters and analyses individual performance trends.
